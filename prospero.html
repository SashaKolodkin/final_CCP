Prospero: Powerful Sorcerer

Bio

Prospero, the unjustly exiled Duke of Milan, uses magic and manipulation to plot deadly revenge against those who wronged him, but ultimately choosing to forgive and let go.

Key Quotes

"We are such stuff as dreams are made on; and our little life is rounded with a sleep."

Character Analysis

Prospero's charachter is volitile and fluid. Transitioning from furious tyrant to humbeled father as the play progresses, his journey shows great chrachter varience.

Role in The Tempest

As the central character, Prospero drives the play's plot with his schemes to regain his dukedom and secure a prosperous future for Miranda.
