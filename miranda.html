Miranda: Innocence

Bio

Miranda is Prospero's naive and compassionate daughter, raised in isolation on the island, and unaware of her noble origins.

Key Quotes

"O brave new world, That has such people in't!"

Character Analysis

Miranda's innocence and naivety are offer a contrast to the deceit and manipulation elsewhere in the play.

Role in The Tempest

Miranda's romance with Ferdinand serve as a catalyst for Prospero's redemption and the play's resolution.
